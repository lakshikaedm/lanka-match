# Moderation Policy

This document explains how Lanka Match handles user reports, safety concerns, and platform moderation. The goal of moderation is to maintain a respectful and safe environment for all users.

## 1. Purpose of Moderation

The platform uses a combination of automated systems and manual review to:

- Prevent harassment and abuse
- Detect scams or fraudulent behavior
- Enforce community standards
- Protect user safety

## 2. Reporting System

Users can report profiles, messages, or behavior that violate the platform rules.

When a report is submitted, the following information may be collected:

- Reported user ID
- Reporting user ID
- Message content or screenshots
- Reason for the report
- Timestamp and system logs

All reports are securely stored in the platform database.

## 3. Moderation Workflow

The moderation system may follow this process:

User submits report  
↓  
Report stored in database  
↓  
Automated moderation system reviews the content  
↓  
AI model analyzes text or images and suggests an action  
↓  
System applies moderation rules or flags for manual review

Automated systems are designed to assist moderation but final actions may be confirmed by platform administrators when necessary.

## 4. Possible Moderation Actions

Depending on the severity of the violation, the system may apply one or more of the following actions:

- Warning to the user
- Temporary messaging restriction
- Temporary account suspension
- Permanent account ban

Repeat violations may result in stronger penalties.

## 5. AI-Assisted Moderation

The platform may use AI systems to help review reported content.

AI tools may analyze:

- Message text
- Images or screenshots
- Behavioral patterns
- Spam or scam indicators

AI suggestions help prioritize moderation decisions but may not always be the final authority.

## 6. Safety and Abuse Prevention

The moderation system aims to detect and prevent:

- Harassment
- Hate speech
- Sexual exploitation
- Fraud and scams
- Impersonation
- Automated bot activity

Accounts engaging in these behaviors may be restricted or removed from the platform.

## 7. Appeals

Users who believe moderation actions were applied incorrectly may request a review through a support or appeals process.

The platform reserves the right to make final moderation decisions to maintain community safety.
