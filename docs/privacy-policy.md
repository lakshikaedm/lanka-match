

# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Lanka Match collects, uses, and protects user information. Because this platform involves personal connections and communication, protecting user privacy is a core priority.

## 1. Data We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

**Profile Information**
- Name or display name
- Age, gender, and profile details
- Photos and other profile content provided by the user

**Messages**
- Messages sent between users on the platform

**Device Information**
- Device type
- Browser information
- Operating system

**IP Address**
- IP address used to access the platform

**Usage Logs**
- Login activity
- Feature usage
- Error logs and system activity

## 2. How Data Is Used

Collected information may be used for the following purposes:

- Matching users and providing core platform features
- Preventing abuse, fraud, and harmful behavior
- Improving the performance and reliability of the platform
- Moderation and safety enforcement

## 3. Data Sharing

User data is not sold to third parties.

However, information may be disclosed:
- If required by law or legal process
- To comply with regulatory obligations
- To protect the safety of users or the integrity of the platform

## 4. Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated personal data.

Please note:
- Certain information may be retained for a limited period for security, fraud prevention, or moderation purposes.
- System logs may also be retained temporarily to maintain platform integrity.
