

# Platform Rules

These rules define acceptable behavior on the platform and help maintain a safe and respectful environment for all users. Violations may result in warnings, restrictions, suspension, or permanent account bans.

## Basic Rules

- Users must be **18 years or older**.
- No harassment, threats, or abusive language.
- No illegal activity.
- No impersonation of another person.
- No selling drugs, scams, or financial schemes.
- No explicit sexual content.
- No spam or advertising.
- Respect other users' boundaries.

## Account Rules

- One account per person.
- Fake photos or stolen identities are not allowed.
- Accounts may be suspended if reported and verified to violate platform rules.

## Messaging Rules

- Messaging is allowed **only after a match** for free users.
- Premium users may send **one introductory message** before matching.

## Moderation Note

These rules will later form the basis of the platform's moderation logic, including automated detection and review of reported behavior.
